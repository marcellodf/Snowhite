    hello

    My name is Margi de Frietas and I run the Snowhite Laundrette, my services include washing, drying, ironing, folding, mending and house cleaning. I am also a depot for dry cleaning services, and dyeing, or bleaching, of clothes. I am very versatile and arrangements can be made for deliveries or pick up times out of my usual working hours. I do household laundry, as well as industrial laundry such as restaurants, guest houses, schools, etc.. I provide laundry services for all walk-in customers, and welcome regular customers- as well as those whom need my services in the case of an emergency.
